Privacy Policy for FSmoking

Effective Date: July 03, 2026

FSmoking is developed by Sankalp Sachan. This Privacy Policy explains how user data is handled.

Information Collection

FSmoking does not collect, upload, sell, or share personal information.

The app stores data locally on your device including:

  • Cigarette tracking records
  • Smoking history
  • User settings
  • Goals

Data Storage

All information remains stored locally on the user's device. No data is transferred to external servers.

Data Export

Users can export their own smoking records using the CSV export feature.

Data Deletion

Users can delete their data anytime from inside the app. Uninstalling the application removes stored data.

Health Disclaimer

FSmoking is a habit tracking tool. It does not provide medical advice.

Children's Privacy

FSmoking is not intended for children under 13.
