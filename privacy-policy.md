# Privacy Policy for FSmoking

**Effective Date: July 03, 2026**

FSmoking ("the app") is developed by Sankalp Sachan.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the FSmoking mobile application.

## Information Collection

FSmoking does not collect, store, or share any personal information with the developer.

The app stores user-created data locally on the user's device, including:

- Cigarette tracking records
- Smoking history
- User preferences
- Goals and settings

This information remains on the user's device.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally using Android device storage.

FSmoking does not upload your smoking history or personal data to external servers.

## Internet Usage

FSmoking does not require an internet connection for core functionality.

## Third Party Services

The app may use Android system services and libraries required for normal functionality.

No personal user information is sold or shared.

## Data Export

Users may export their own tracking data using the CSV export feature.

Exported files remain under the user's control.

## Data Deletion

Users can delete their data anytime using the reset/delete option available inside the app.

Uninstalling the application also removes locally stored app data.

## Health Disclaimer

FSmoking is a habit tracking application.

Information shown in the app is for motivation and awareness only and does not replace professional medical advice.

## Children's Privacy

FSmoking is not designed for children under 13 years of age.

## Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when necessary.

Updates will be posted on this page.
