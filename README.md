# 📖 About

**FSmoking** is a native Android quit-smoking companion app designed to help users understand and improve their smoking habits.

The app focuses on:

✔ Habit awareness  
✔ Cigarette consumption tracking  
✔ Smoke-free progress  
✔ Money saving motivation  
✔ Health improvement milestones  

> Small improvements every day create long term change.

# ✨ Features


## 🚬 Smoking Tracker

- Add cigarette entries instantly
- Track daily cigarette usage
- View complete smoking history
- Search smoking records


---

## ⏱ Smoke-Free Timer

Real-time progress tracking:

- Hours
- Minutes
- Seconds

since your last cigarette.


---

## 📊 Analytics Dashboard

Monitor:

- Daily cigarettes
- Weekly statistics
- Monthly progress
- Average smoking interval
- Spending calculation


---

## 💡 Smart Insights

Understand your habits:

🔥 Peak smoking hour

📅 Highest smoking day

⏱ Average smoking interval


---

## ❤️ Health Dashboard

Track recovery milestones:

❤️ Heart rate improvement

🫁 Oxygen improvement

🌬 Breathing recovery

🚭 Nicotine reduction timeline
