# Privacy Policy for FSmoking

**Effective Date:** July 03, 2026

FSmoking is developed and maintained by **Sankalp Sachan**.

This Privacy Policy explains how FSmoking handles user information when using the mobile application.

By using FSmoking, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

FSmoking does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as:

- Name
- Address
- Phone number
- Contacts
- Location data

The app allows users to enter information related to their smoking habits for personal tracking purposes, including:

- Cigarette records
- Smoking history
- Quit progress
- Personal goals
- App preferences and settings

This information is stored only on the user's device.

FSmoking does not upload this data to any external server.

---

## 2. How User Data Is Stored

All user-created data is stored locally on the device using Android storage.

This includes:

- Tracking records
- Statistics
- User preferences
- Progress information

The developer does not have access to this information.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

The information stored inside the app is used only to provide app features such as:

- Displaying smoking history
- Showing progress statistics
- Calculating smoke-free progress
- Tracking goals
- Displaying achievements

Your information is never used for advertising or profiling.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

FSmoking does not:

- Sell user data
- Share user data with third parties
- Transfer personal information
- Use user information for marketing purposes

Your data remains under your control.

---

## 5. Internet Access

FSmoking's main features work without requiring an internet connection.

The application may use standard Android services or libraries required for normal app functionality.

---

## 6. Data Export

FSmoking may provide options to export your personal tracking data.

Exported files are created only when requested by the user.

Users are responsible for storing and managing exported files.

---

## 7. Data Deletion

Users can delete their data at any time by:

- Using the delete/reset option inside the app
- Clearing application data from Android settings
- Uninstalling the application

Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.

---

## 8. Security

We respect user privacy and aim to keep user information safe.

Since all data is stored locally, protecting access to your device helps protect your information.

No electronic storage method can guarantee complete security.

---

## 9. Health Disclaimer

FSmoking is a habit tracking and motivational application.

The app is not a medical application and does not provide:

- Medical diagnosis
- Treatment advice
- Professional healthcare recommendations

Information displayed inside the app is for awareness and motivation purposes only.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding health concerns.

## 10. Children's Privacy

FSmoking is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
